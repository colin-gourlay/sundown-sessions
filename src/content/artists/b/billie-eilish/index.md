## About

American singer-songwriter, born on December 18, 2001, in Los Angeles, California. Sister of Finneas O'Connell.
Her first single "[Ocean Eyes](https://www.discogs.com/master/1574747-Billie-Eilish-Ocean-Eyes)" was released in 2016 when she was 14 years old.
She released her E.P. "[Dont Smile At Me](https://www.discogs.com/master/1240011-Billie-Eilish-Dont-Smile-At-Me)" in August 2017 and her first album "[When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?](https://www.discogs.com/master/1524311-Billie-Eilish-When-We-All-Fall-Asleep-Where-Do-We-Go)" in March 2019, which would go on to become a phenomenal success for the 17-year-old artist.
Her downtempo second album, "[Happier Than Ever](https://www.discogs.com/master/2234794-Billie-Eilish-Happier-Than-Ever)", released in July 2021, delves into self-reflection of the rise-to-fame she faced in her previous album and the downsides of stardom that came with it.
Her latest album, "[Hit Me Hard And Soft](https://www.discogs.com/master/3487663-Billie-Eilish-Hit-Me-Hard-And-Soft)" was released in May 2024.
