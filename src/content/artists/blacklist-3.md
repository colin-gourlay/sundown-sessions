## About

Blacklist were a Street punk / Hardcore punk band formed in Denver, Colorado around the late 1990's. 
Band members: Vocals: J.R. Spiegel , Bass: Eric Sparks, Drums: Josh Tolve, Guitar: Nate Stone, Guitar: Sean Brailey
