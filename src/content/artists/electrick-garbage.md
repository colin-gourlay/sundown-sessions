## About

Punk Rock band from Grenoble (France) formed in 1977

Original Line-Up :
Jean-Jacques Archinard (Vocals)
Philippe Marciano (Guitar / Vocals)
Yvon Astoux (Bass)
Pierre Crozet (Drums)

Additional members :
Jacques Ivry (Drums)
Bernard Rey (Bass)
Dominique Marceau (Bass)
