## About

Rock and roll band from Kikinda (Yugoslavia, now Serbia) formed in 1989. Disbanded in December 1991.

Founding members:
Zlatko Sandić - drums
Kristijan Sestak - bass
Miodrag Blažić - guitar
Vladimir Jovičić - vocal

Bane Rackov - guitar
Slobodan Kličković - bass
Voja Đukić - drums
