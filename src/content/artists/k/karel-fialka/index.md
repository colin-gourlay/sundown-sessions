## About

Electro artist and poet, born in Bengal, India to a Czech father & Scottish mother. In 1979, his Indie release [Armband](http://www.discogs.com/Karel-Fialka-Armband/master/523208) on the Red Shift label became a cult classic. Then in 1980 he had a radio hit with the song [The Eyes Have It](http://www.discogs.com/master/152228), which has been featured on many New Wave collections, and still has a lot of airplay. This was taken from his critically well-received album [Still Life](http://www.discogs.com/master/94143) on the Blueprint label. Another release from this album was (the almost prophetic) a standalone single.

It was to be another eight years before his next major release, though he was a very active songwriter during the time and numerous artists have covered his songs. 1988 brought Karel his biggest hit, the catchy and serious [Hey Matthew](http://www.discogs.com/master/152230) on Miles Copeland's I.R.S. records. The follow up album was [Human Animal](http://www.discogs.com/master/94144), containing more quirky glimpses of life from Karel's unique point of view. Two other singles were released from the album, [Eat, Drink, Dance, Relax](http://www.discogs.com/Karel-Fialka-Eat-Drink-Dance-Relax/master/152229) and another single. The [CD version](http://www.discogs.com/release/1989441) of the album included extended mixes including 2 new versions of the 1980 single "The Eyes Have It".

Karel is still writing and recording with other bands and artists, as well as being deeply immersed in production duties. He has been at work on his third album for sometime, a dark hommage which was released in 2009 as digital download, or in two hard copy formats from karelfialka.com .
