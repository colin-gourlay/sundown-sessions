## About

British musical act, and dance troupe formed in 1979. Due to their extravagant costumes (designed by Kahn and Bell) and performances, and their connection with the Blitz Club (London), they quickly found themselves at the forefront of the New Romantic movement of the early-1980s. Having secured a record deal with RCA they released a couple of singles, and supported several acts on tour.

Upon the departure of Pereno and Richards in 1981, they were reduced to a 4-piece. A robotic performance art duo who had simultaneously been part of the group since 1980, left in 1982. It was with their departure that the act came to an end.

**Original Members**
Tim Dry (1979-82)
Barbie Wilde (1979-82)
Robert Pereno (1979-81)
Lowri-Ann Richards (1979-81)
