## About

Five-piece punk rock n roll band from Decatur, Illinois. Formed in 2008.
Band members:
Billy Fury (Vocals)
Cliff Strangler (Guitar/Vocals)
Sammy Strangler (Guitar)
Danny Strangler (Bass/Vocals)
Beanz (Drums)
