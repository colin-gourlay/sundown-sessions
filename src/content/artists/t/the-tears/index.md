## About

British rock duo formed in London in 2004, comprising Brett Anderson (vocals) and Bernard Butler (guitar). Both had previously been members of Suede until Butler's departure in 1994. The pair reunited a decade later to record their only studio album, "Here Come The Tears" (2005), before disbanding in 2006.
