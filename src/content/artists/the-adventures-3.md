## About

Swedish sixties rock band from Stockholm. 

Not to be confused with the other Swedish group, [a711327]. 

Members were:
Bill Samuelsson: Guitar / vocals
Jörgen Pettersson: Guitar
Kent Kolström: Bas guitar
Björn Wallin: Drums

Lennart "Lenne" Broberg: Vocals
Staffan Stenström: bas guitar
Andy Williams: Vocals
Bo Rosén:  Guitar
Claes "Mecki" Bodemark: Organ
