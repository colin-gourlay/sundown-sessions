## About

Claimed to be the Beatles from a parallel universe in which they never broke up.
The 'Everyday Chemistry' release origins from an unknown source whom prefers to be called James Richards.
The audio content from that release contains remixed samples from the solo careers of [a46481], [a35301], [a243955] and [a259352].

James Richards wrote a fictional narrative where he was taken to another dimension on the 9th of september 2009.
Within this alternate dimension the Beatles never broke up and released a few more albums.
He managed to grab a cassette copy of 'Everyday Chemistry' before returning to our dimension.
