## About

Acoustic rock band from Abingdon, Oxfordshire, UK
formed in 1994, disbanded on September 16, 2000.

Line-up: 
Andy Yorke (vocals, guitar - brother of Thom Yorke, Radiohead)
Jason Moulster (bass)
Nigel Powell (drums)
Jim Crosskey
