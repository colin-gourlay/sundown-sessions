## About

Yeah Yeah Yeahs are a New York City-based alternative rock band founded in 2000. The band consists of lead singer Karen O, drummer Brian Chase, and guitarist Nick Zinner.

### Current Lineup

Nick Zinner, Karen O, Brian Chase
