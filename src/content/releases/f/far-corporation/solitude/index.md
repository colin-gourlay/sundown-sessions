## About

Solitude is an album by Far Corporation, the multinational supergroup assembled by German producer Frank Farian. The project featured musicians from Toto among others, and became notable for recording the first charting cover version of Led Zeppelin's 'Stairway to Heaven', which reached number eight in the UK in October 1985.
