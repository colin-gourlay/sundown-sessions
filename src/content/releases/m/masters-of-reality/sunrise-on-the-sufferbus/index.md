## About

Sunrise on the Sufferbus is the third studio album by American rock group Masters of Reality, released on Chrysalis Records in 1992. The album was produced by Ginger Baker and features the band's characteristic blend of blues, psychedelia, and heavy rock. It is considered one of their most accomplished and eclectic works.
